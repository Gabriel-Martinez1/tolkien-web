The World of Arda

"All that is gold does not glitter,
Not all those who wander are lost;
The old that is strong does not wither,
Deep roots are not reached by the frost.

From the ashes a fire shall be woken,
A light from the shadows shall spring;
Renewed shall be blade that was broken,
The crownless again shall be king."

— J.R.R. Tolkien, The Fellowship of the Ring

Arda is the world created by Eru Ilúvatar, shaped by the music of the Ainur and home to Elves, Men, Dwarves, and many other peoples. From the ancient days of the Ainulindalë to the end of the Third Age, this site explores the rich histories, geographies, and legends of Middle-earth and beyond.
